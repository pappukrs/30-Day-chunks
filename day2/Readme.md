# 🟢 Day 2: Standup Format

🎯 **Aaj ka Goal:** Daily standup mein confident aur clear feel karna by using structured, predictable chunks.

---

## 🗣️ Warm Up
**Pehle in phrases ko 3 baar out loud (zor se) bolo:**

> *"I'm working on..."* <br/>
> *"Right now my focus is..."* <br/>
> *"Yesterday I worked on..."* <br/>
> *"Today I'm going to..."* <br/>
> *"I have a blocker — ..."* <br/>
> *"No blockers for now."*

---

## 🛠️ Standup Format: Simple Structure

Standups mein zyada words dhoondne ki zarurat nahi hai, sirf ek simple 3-part structure follow karna hota hai:

- ⬅️ **Yesterday** → What you did
- ➡️ **Today** → What you will do
- 🚫 **Blocker** → Stuck on something? Ya phir simply bolo *"No blockers for now."*

---

## 📝 Ab Practice Karte Hain!

Jo tu Super App for Ashva pe kaam kar raha hai (jiske bare me Day 1 me bataya), uske basis par apna ek simple daily standup update try kar.

Jaise ki:
- *"Yesterday I worked on..."*
- *"Today I'm going to..."*
- *"No blockers for now."* (or agar koi blocker hai, toh use explain kar)

👇 **Apna standup update yaha likho ya bolo:**

---

### 🎙️ My Standup Update (Practice)

- ⬅️ **Yesterday:** I worked on adding a new calendar view feature for technician scheduling, so coordinators can view it easily. 
- 🚫 **Blocker:** I have a blocker — I didn't have access to the sales app codebase, so I'm waiting on that before I can integrate it into the Super App. 
- ➡️ **Today:** I'm working on fixing some bugs related to the category view in the Super App.
