# 🟢 Day 1: "Explaining Your Work"

🎯 **Aaj ka Goal:** Apne current work ko explain karne ke liye 2-3 "chunks" (ready-made phrases) ke sath comfortable feel karna.

---

## 🧠 Aaj ke Chunks 
*(Inhe samjho aur yaad karo)*

1. **"So basically what I'm doing is..."**
2. **"The idea here is..."**
3. **"So what I'm building is..."**

---

## 🗣️ Warm Up 
**Pehle inhe sirf bolo**

In phrases ko **3 baar out loud (zor se) bolo**. Abhi meaning ya grammar par focus mat karo, sirf apne tongue ko English bolne ke liye comfortable karo:

> *"So basically what I'm doing is..."* <br/>
> *"The idea here is..."* <br/>
> *"So what I'm building is..."*
